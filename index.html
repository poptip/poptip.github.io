Our Stack

Our back-ends are written almost entirely in Go (some Node processes handle our visualizations) and our front-ends are written primarily with AngularJS. We use MongoDB, Redis, and PostgreSQL for storage/caching and Storm for stream processing. It all hums along nicely on AWS, with every engineer required to be knowledgeable and productive across all levels of the stack.
